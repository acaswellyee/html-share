V3 Design Decisions
Two deliberate choices kept taxonomy lean: (1) Healthcare spend folded into HR > Occupational Health Services — this client is not a healthcare company; all spend is employee/workforce-related. (2) Construction & Renovation kept inside FACILITIES rather than elevated to its own top-level category. ~20–25 vendors, mostly project-based capex. Facilities managers own these relationships. Filterable via subcategory without taxonomy bloat. Revisit if annual spend exceeds ~$5M.
HR > Occupational Health Services FACILITIES > Construction & Renovation MEAT/SEAFOOD > Lamb/Exotic Meats PACKAGING > Insulated Shippers
Audit Findings
Missing Nodes
7 gaps found
Plant-Based & Alternative Proteins
No node for Beyond Meat, Impossible, cultured meat. Confirmed by a confirmed plant-based vendor in vendor data. Created as standalone Subcategory1 — keeps it separate from animal protein to avoid AI confusion.
FOOD/INGREDIENTS › PLANT-BASED & ALT. PROTEINS › PLANT-BASED MEAT SUBSTITUTES
Construction & Renovation
~20–25 vendors confirmed: regional construction vendors confirmed in vendor data Folded into FACILITIES rather than elevated — project-based spend, facilities managers own these relationships.
FACILITIES › CONSTRUCTION & RENOVATION › GENERAL CONTRACTING / ROOFING / HVAC…
Occupational Health Services
confirmed occupational health vendors — all employee health, not clinical ops. Consolidated under HR rather than a new top-level HEALTHCARE category.
HR › OCCUPATIONAL HEALTH SERVICES › INJURY MANAGEMENT
Lamb / Exotic Meats
confirmed exotic protein vendors were being misclassified as BEEF. Lamb and bison are distinct protein categories. Added LAMB/EXOTIC MEATS as a child of MEAT/SEAFOOD.
FOOD/INGREDIENTS › MEAT/SEAFOOD › LAMB/EXOTIC MEATS
Insulated Shippers / Cold-Chain Packaging
confirmed cold-chain packaging vendors all appeared in vendor data with this subcategory — but it didn't exist in the taxonomy. Critical for the client's DTC model.
PACKAGING › SHIPPING SUPPLIES › INSULATED SHIPPERS/COLD-CHAIN PKG
Marinades & Rubs
Direct input for marinated products. No existing CONDIMENTS node covered this. confirmed seasoning vendors are partially covered under SPICES & SEASONINGS but marinades are distinct.
FOOD/INGREDIENTS › CONDIMENTS › MARINADES & RUBS
Gift Boxes / Premium Packaging + E-Commerce Platform
Two DTC-specific gaps: branded gift box packaging (core to the client's gifting business) and an e-commerce platform node distinct from the catch-all IT SOFTWARE.
PACKAGING › GIFT BOXES/PREMIUM PACKAGING
IT/TELECOM › E-COMMERCE PLATFORM
Overlap Issues Resolved
8 conflicts fixed
DAIRY & EGG PRODUCTS ↔ specific dairy nodes
Catch-all sat alongside BUTTER/CREAMERY, MILK, CHEESE — any dairy invoice could land in 2+ places. AI would split inconsistently.
Renamed to EGGS & EGG PRODUCTS only
IT/TELECOM STAFFING ↔ HR IT STAFFING
Exact duplicate in two categories. Same vendor, same invoice — completely random classification outcome.
IT/TELECOM › STAFFING node removed; route to HR
SOY & SOY PRODUCTS ↔ DAIRY ALTERNATIVES
Soy milk, tofu, soy-based drinks fit both. No context available to AI at invoice level.
SOY node scoped to "RAW/INDUSTRIAL INPUTS ONLY"
JAN-SAN/CLEANING ↔ JANITORIAL SERVICES
Products vs. services — a logical distinction AI can't reliably make without explicit labeling. Confirmed by vendors: confirmed product vendors vs. confirmed service vendors.
Added "(PRODUCTS ONLY)" and "(SERVICES ONLY)" tags
REFRIGERATED WAREHOUSING ↔ FOOD WAREHOUSING
Cold food storage belongs to both. confirmed cold storage vendors — all correctly merge to single node.
Merged → REFRIGERATED/FOOD WAREHOUSING
DIGITAL MARKETING ↔ SEO ↔ SOCIAL MEDIA
SEO and Social/Influencer are sub-disciplines of Digital Marketing, not peers. Same vendor (confirmed digital advertising platforms) could land in 3 different nodes.
SEO and SOCIAL MEDIA nested under DIGITAL MARKETING
OFFICE FURNITURE (Purchase) ↔ FURNITURE (Repair)
Buying furniture vs. repairing it — an AI can't distinguish without explicit label context. Two separate nodes, same name.
Labels clarified with (PURCHASE) and (REPAIR ONLY)
confirmed exotic protein vendors miscategorized as BEEF
Data quality issue in vendor file — bison and lamb mapped to BEEF, and one vendor had a row mapped to MEAT/SEAFOOD at subcategory1 level (schema error).
LAMB/EXOTIC MEATS node added; vendor data to be corrected
